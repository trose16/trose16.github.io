"Was Great Aunt Edna married in 1910 or 1914? What name is her marriage certificate filed under?

Organizing genealogy information can be frustrating. Is the information filed by event, location, name? Having to search through multiple files is time consuming and frusterating. But what if you could easily search through all your family history files to find that ancestor who lived in London in the 1850's?

With GEO all you have to do is enter names, dates, and places; then let GEO adds searchable tags. This works with photos, text, and documents.
