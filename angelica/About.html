Board Members

Our Board Members are all volunteers who have a passion to help the Latino Community. A few were part of the original 6 that started this amazing festival over 20 years ago.

Volunteers

Our festival would not be possible without our VOLUNTEERS! From set-up, trash-duty, to clean-up, they help us keep things running smoothly. If you would like to volunteer click here.

Community

We started the festival to help bridge the gap between community services and the community. Our partners help meet the needs of the community, and our job is to provide a venue where the two can meet.
