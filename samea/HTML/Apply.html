Are you like many of us women and mothers, trying to find a place of belonging and opportunities that match our worth? With Tech-Moms, mothers and women of all ages, backgrounds, ethnicities and more are coming together to build an overwhelmingly supportive and empowering community for those who are curious about entering the tech world. Through part-time training programs they provide students the opportunity to build the skills, knowledge, and professional network needed to transition into or re-enter a career in tech.

We are now accepting applications for Winter classes which will begin January 2023.
